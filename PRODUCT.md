# Allianz - Area Personale (Fondo Pensione)

This project is a mobile-responsive, high-fidelity client app and scraping agent designed to provide an interface for managing Allianz Pension Funds, aligned with the actual corporate branding of Allianz.it.

## Design Context

- **Register**: product
- **Vibe/Tone**: Trustworthy, corporate, clean, highly professional. It should feel like an official Allianz tool, not an AI template.
- **Theme**: Light-first design, matching `allianz.it`'s styling language.
- **Font Family**: 'Allianz Neo', sans-serif (no generic 'Inter' font stack).
- **Core Color Palette**:
  - Primary Brand Blue: `#003781` (Allianz Blue / Blue 900)
  - Dark Accent Blue: `#00266D` (Blue 1000)
  - Focus/Active Blue: `#001955` (Blue 1100)
  - Subtle/Emphasis Background: `#F0F7FC` (Blue 100)
  - Background Neutral: `#FFFFFF`
  - Body Text Neutral: `#1a1a2e` (ink)
  - Muted Label/Text: `#5a6a7e`
  - Border Neutral: `rgba(0, 55, 129, 0.1)`

## Key Design Principles (Impeccable & Allianz)

1. **No AI Design Tropes**: No generic purple/blue gradients. No floating glassmorphic cards with blur/glow. Everything should look like premium corporate design.
2. **Proper Spacing**: Leverage the 4px vertical rhythm grid. Clear margins and spacing for readable lists.
3. **Contrast & Typography**: Main body text must meet or exceed WCAG AA contrast rules (>= 4.5:1). Label colors are dark enough to read easily.
4. **Mobile First & Responsive Layouts**:
   - The interactive tables and operational forms must fit comfortably on a mobile screen.
   - Avoid long tables or grids overflowing horizontally on mobile viewports.
   - Touch targets for buttons, inputs, and links must be at least 44x44px to make them easily tappable on phones.
   - Form inputs must be full-width on mobile.
